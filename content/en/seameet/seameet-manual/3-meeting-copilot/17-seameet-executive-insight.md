## 1. Prerequisites

Before enabling Executive Insights, ensure you have:

* A registered account at [SeaMeet.ai](https://seameet.ai).
* The **SeaMeet Chrome Extension** installed: [Download here](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/seameet-take-chatgpt-meet/gkkhkniggakfgioeeclbllpihmipkcmn).

## 2. Setting Up Executive Insights

To activate the daily briefing for your leadership team:

1. **Access the Dashboard:** Log in to your SeaMeet account.
2. **Navigate to Feature:** Click on **Executive Insights** in the left-hand sidebar.
3. **Enable Reports:** Go to the **Settings** tab.
4. **Toggle Activation:** Switch the "Daily Reports" toggle to **ON**.
5. **Configure Preferences:**
* **Language & Time Zone:** Set your preferred language (e.g., English) and local time zone to ensure reports arrive at the start of your workday.
* **Manage Recipients:** Add the email addresses of stakeholders and leaders who should receive the report.
* **Schedule:** Select which days of the week (e.g., Mon–Fri) you wish to receive the intelligence briefing.



## 3. Understanding the Daily Report

The report is delivered via email and accessible through the dashboard. It contains six key intelligence modules:

* **Strategic Signals:** AI-identified market shifts and high-intent buying signals.
* **Tactical Action Items:** A cross-functional checklist of every commitment made across all meetings.
* **Sales & Customer Pulse:** Unfiltered feedback and objections directly from the "voice of the customer."
* **Blockers & Risks:** Immediate flagging of hurdles to prevent minor delays from becoming major setbacks.
* **Team Sentiment Snapshot:** An aggregate view of team morale and burnout risks across hundreds of conversations.
* **CEO Suggestion of the Day:** An AI-generated, high-level strategic recommendation based on the day's total data.

## 4. Key Benefits

* **100% Meeting Coverage:** Eliminates organizational "blind spots."
* **Rapid Issue Identification:** Identify critical issues 3x faster than traditional reporting.
* **Total Visibility:** Transforms siloed data into a unified road map for leadership.
